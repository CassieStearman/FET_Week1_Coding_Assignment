We use an eclectic selection of resources to educate our students. Below is a list of some of the resources we use.

Math

Math Mammoth

Math Mammoth is an excellent program for our kindergarten, first, and second grade students. The lessons are comprehensive and provide a solid foundation to build on.

Math Mammath

Beast Academy

Beast Academy by Art of Problem Solving is a rigorous program for third through sixth grade that encourages students to think outside the box and to have fun with mathamatics.

Beast Academy

Language Arts

Logic of English - Foundations

Logic of English Foundations is centered around phonograms and spelling rules to help students develop a deeper understanding of the english language for grades kindergarten through second.

Logic of English

Michael Clay Thompson's English Language Arts

Michael Clay Thompson's classical English language arts curriculum is all about socratic discussion and the idea that meaningless busy work only does harm to a childs education. This is used for grades third through eighth.

MCT Language Arts Curriculum

Science

Real Science Odyssey

Real Science Odyssey by Pandia Press takes a hands on approach to science. Our studets gain critical thinking, and problem solving skills through weekly science expiriments. Used in all grade levels.

Real Science Odyssey

History

History Odyssey

History odyssey, by Pandia Press, makes history fun and interactive through storytelling and hands on activities. Used in all grade levels.

History Odyssey

Physical Education

Cosmic Kids Yoga

Our students enjoy excercise through the lens of the great outdoors. They have access to Hiking, biking, kyaking, rock climbing, and much more. In addition to our outdoors activities our students practice yoga to keep
the body and mind fit and flexible.

Cosmic Kids Yoga

Language

DuoLingo

DuoLingo is an excellent language learning platform that turns learning into a game.

DuoLingo
